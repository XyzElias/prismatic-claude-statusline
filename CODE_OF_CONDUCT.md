# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Prismatic to be a friendly, welcoming project for everyone, regardless of experience level or background.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; give helpful, specific feedback.
- Keep discussion focused on the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory remarks.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or PRs that violate this code, and may block repeat offenders. To report a concern, contact the maintainer privately via a GitHub issue requesting a contact channel.

This is a lightweight adaptation of the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
